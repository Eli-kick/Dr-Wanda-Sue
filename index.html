About Dr. WandaSue

Welcome! Join us here to immerse yourself in both adult and children's stories that will take you to places you loved from your past — or are learning to enjoy as a part of today and the future.

From adult Christian western romances that pulse with the energy of cutting-horse competitions, to heartwarming children's books that introduce young readers to the magic of the horse world — Dr. WandaSue writes with warmth, faith, and purpose.
